## Honnaka (本中)
**Founded:** December 2010
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (Outvision Group / CA Co., Ltd. → WILL)
**Headquarters:** Ebisu, Shibuya Ward, Tokyo
**Name meaning:** 本中 = abbreviation of 本物中出し (honmono nakadashi) — "genuine/real creampie." Original tagline: "ゴム禁！ギジ禁！ナマ解禁！！" ("Condoms prohibited! Fakes prohibited! Raw only!!")
**Specialty:** Creampie (中出し, nakadashi) content across wide scenario variety — amateur, NTR, scenario-driven, and gonzo. Positioned as a "comprehensive planning maker" with strong scenario diversity layered on the creampie conceit. Releases on the 25th of each month.
**Early history:** Nearly went bankrupt in early years — one more year of losses would have meant closure. Turning point: 2013, when Ai Uehara sought unconventional projects and signed with Honnaka. Her involvement drew industry attention and rapidly elevated the label to one of WILL's highest-volume operations.
**Sub-labels:** HNJC (Jukunaka — mature/MILF), HNKY (Nichijou — scenario/project series), HNSE (amateur sub-series), HNTV (variety format), KRND (Kyousei — coercion/gangbang, est. July 2013)
**Notable actresses:** Ai Uehara (pivotal 2013 signing, Honnaka Award 2014–2015, credited with saving the label); Shiina Sora (2016 Award); Asahara Tsubasa (2017); Miyazaki Aya (2018); Jinguji Nao (2019); Matsumoto Ichika (exclusive Nov 2023, dedicated sub-label created, 2024 Award)
**Notable series:** "First Raw Creampie" (100+ titles, evergreen flagship); "Girlfriend's Younger Sister NTR" (50+ titles, 2015–2024); "Beautiful Girl Creampie Island" (anniversary series since 2013, relaunched 2024); "College Drinking Party Creampie Gang Rape Circle" (15+ titles, 2017–2020)
**Awards:** Annual Honnaka Award presented at MOODYZ group year-end events since 2014
**Notes:** Three-pillar production strategy documented by studio: "sales-focused works," "series with capable supporting actresses," "entertainment-focused projects regardless of sales." KRND sub-label operates independently from the main romantic/relationship HMN content — a separate gangbang/coercion flavor.

Flagship series focuses on "M-type male" scenarios.
**Notable actresses:** Rin Sakuragi (primary face of Bi era, appeared in AV Open 2015 submission); Claire Hasumi; Sho Nishino; Mami Yuuki; Ichika Matsumoto (current exclusive, listed 2024)

## WILL/JHV Group
**Founded:** Alice Japan label est. April 5, 1986; parent Japan Home Video (JHV) est. May 1984; MAX-A est. May 1992
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation); group originated within the Kuki network
**What "JHV" stands for:** Japan Home Video (日本ホームビデオ) — the parent company of Alice Japan, established May 1984 to capitalize on the VCR boom. JHV created the Alice Japan AV label in 1986.
**Specialty:** Classic-era flagship and idol content. Alice Japan is one of Japan's oldest continuously operating AV labels (1986). MAX-A brought premium idol-class production and was the proving ground for Goro Tameike (began directing the Samantha sub-label in 1994 before moving to SOD → Moodyz → independence). Together these labels represent the full arc of early-to-peak-era JAV idol production.
**Constituent labels:**
- **Alice Japan** (DV/DVAJ codes): General AV; first major star Hitomi Kobayashi (1986+); Yuma Asami debut October 28, 2005 (DVAJ-001 "Innocent Hard-Core"), ranked #1 DMM actress sales 2006 and 2007. Sub-labels: SERO (EROTICA spin-off), KIRA (Kirameki spin-off).
- **MAX-A Karen** (XC/XV/SRXV/XVSR codes): Premium idol-class content, launched May 23, 1992. Roster included: Yua Aida (debut 2004), Rio/Tina Yuzuki (debut 2005), Sora Aoi, Akiho Yoshizawa, Bunko Kanazawa, Kyoko Kazama, Mihiro, Hitomi Hayasaka. Sub-labels: PXV (max pink reprint), UPSM (UP'S spin-off), WHX (Whip spin-off).
- **MAX-A Samantha** (XS code): Distinct sub-brand within MAX-A; origin label for Goro Tameike's career (1994).
**Kuki group context:** By ~1997, the Kuki group (KUKI Inc. + Alice Japan + MAX-A + Atlas21 + Media Station + Big Morkal + Sexia) was the largest family of AV companies in Japan, all sharing the X CITY internet distribution infrastructure (one of the first major AV web platforms, launched 1995). This group predated and was eventually absorbed into the Hokuto/WILL distribution network.
**Notable actresses:** Hitomi Kobayashi, Yuma Asami, Yua Aida, Rio/Tina Yuzuki, Sora Aoi, Akiho Yoshizawa, Bunko Kanazawa, Kyoko Kazama, Mihiro, Hitomi Hayasaka, Riko Tachibana
**Notes:** Goro Tameike's origin in MAX-A Samantha (1994) is a historically significant thread — the foundational director of modern jukujo AV learned his craft here before building a career at SOD → Moodyz → his own label. Yumika Hayashi also connects the JHV world to WILL — she debuted at h.m.p (bidet wheel group, 1989) before becoming a Madonna star.
**Sources:** Alice Japan Wikipedia; Japan Home Video Wikipedia; Max-A Wikipedia; Goro Tameike Wikipedia; Yuma Asami Wikipedia

## bidet wheel (ビデ倫 / Biderin / NEVA)
**Group name origin:** "bidet wheel" is a machine-translation artifact for ビデ倫 (Biderin) = Nihon Ethics of Video Association (NEVA) — the Japanese adult video industry self-regulatory body. The grouping collects labels whose parent studios share NEVA membership. These are NOT a corporate entity — they are independent companies that shared association membership.
**Status:** h.m.p. active; KUKI Inc. dissolved February 22, 2017; TMA active
**Constituent studios:**

**h.m.p. (Houyuu Media Produce / 芳友メディアプロデュース)**
- Founded 1981 (as "Samm" / サムビデオ — name taken from S&M, reflecting early content focus)
- One of Japan's oldest continuously active AV companies
- Shifted from S&M to mainstream idol-class content in mid-1980s under directors Kaoru Toyoda, Ryutaro Kanno, Yukihiko Shimamura
- 1984: Miss Christine sub-label created for single-featured-actress videos; Tiffany sub-label followed
- Notable debuts: Keiko Nakazawa (1986); Rena Murakami (Miss Christine, March 1988); Yumika Hayashi (Miss Christine, June 1989 — later won Best Actress at Pink Grand Prix 2005, became Madonna star); Maiko Yuki (Miss Christine — later founding star of Moodyz)
- Code: HODV; also produces adult toys/sex aids under separate brand

**KUKI Inc.**
- Founded 1977 by Noriaki Nakagawa — one of the oldest adult entertainment companies in Japan (predates VCR-era AV)
- Began as adult magazine publisher; released first AV in 1983
- 1995: Launched www.kuki.co.jp — among the first AV companies with a commercial web presence; site became X CITY, which became Japan's largest adult website during peak years
- By ~1997: Built the Kuki group (Alice Japan, MAX-A, Atlas21, Media Station, Big Morkal, Sexia) — largest AV company network in Japan before WILL/SOD/Prestige era
- Code: ADZ; dissolved February 22, 2017
- Context: See WILL/JHV Group entry — KUKI Inc. was the mother company of the Kuki group that Alice Japan and MAX-A operated within

**TMA (Total Media Agency)**
- Founded October 1990, Toshima ward, Tokyo
- Japan's definitive anime/manga/game parody AV studio — parodied properties including Evangelion, Haruhi Suzumiya, Lucky Star, K-On! and many others
- 2005: One of first AV studios to produce UMD format content (PlayStation Portable, 3 videos released August 12, 2005)
- 2007: One of first Japanese AV companies to release Blu-ray content
- Also operates Tamatoys adult toy brand
- Code: AKB (specialty), HITMA ("hit manga" — the parody series code; name is a direct play on "hit manga"), ID

**Notes:** h.m.p's Miss Christine label was a launching pad for Yumika Hayashi and Maiko Yuki — both went on to become major names at other WILL labels. TMA's HITMA code ("Hit Manga") is one of the more self-explanatory product codes in the industry. KUKI's 1977 founding predates AV as a commercial category, making it arguably the oldest company represented in the database.
**Sources:** h.m.p. (Japan) Wikipedia; Kuki Inc. Wikipedia; Total Media Agency Wikipedia; Nihon Ethics of Video Association Wikipedia

## WILL/Delusional Group (妄想族 / Mousouzoku)
**Founded:** April 2006
**Status:** Active (253 affiliated makers as of March 2016)
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (Outvision/CA/Hokuto)
**Name meaning:** 妄想族 (Mousouzoku) = "Delusional Tribe/Clan" — 妄想 (mōsō) = "delusion/fantasy." Tagline: "A direct hit on your vague desires! #1 in participating makers, genres covered, and total titles!!"
**What it is:** NOT a single production company — a marketing and distribution umbrella aggregating hundreds of small, highly specialized independent AV producers under Outvision/WILL's distribution infrastructure (physical + DMM/FANZA digital). WILL's flagship labels serve mainstream idol content; Mousouzoku catches the entire long tail of niche fetishes those labels don't serve.
**Three original divisions:**
- 妄想族 (Main): Broadest niche range; core is Art Body Collection (ABC) with BOBB/OKSN/PTKS codes
- 妄想族 BLACK LABEL: Harder/extreme tier; Art Video (Japan's first SM video company, founded 1982) joined here in 2010
- 妄想族インターナショナル (International): Foreign actress specialty — Partkin (PTKS, blonde/Russian, Gina Gerson recurring), Spartan (ANCI)
**Expansion timeline:** August 2012: merged with Goemon Group. April 2014: absorbed Tokyo Mani Gun's + HERO group. September 2014: integrated Beautiful Witch. October 2014: absorbed Emmanuel Group (still listed separately in database as WILL/Emmanuel Group).
**Core sub-label (Art Body Collection / ABC):** WILL's own in-house production unit within Mousouzoku. Boeing BB (BOBB — busty), Okasan.com (OKSN — mature women/MILF), Partkin (PTKS — Western actresses), JK Five (JKFV — school uniform fetish)
**Key genre coverage:**
- BDSM/SM: Art Video SM (ADVO), AMEDIA (AMEB), Bermuda (BDA — female investigator scenarios), Painblood (PPBB)
- Shemale/crossdresser: Lady Boy (LBOY), Ofenipenny World (OPPW)
- Voyeur: Kurayami (YAMI — massage voyeur), P-Project (DPJT), Koiwa Movie (KOIW)
- Extreme/scat: jam (JAMC)
- Lesbian: Real Vision (LESS)
- Cosplay: Ramakos (COSU — brucera/cosplay), 2.8 Dimensions (TWOE — anime character cosplay)
- Foreign: DIVA (DIVAS — blonde/Black/Asian), Gold Emperor (GEMR — Western style), Asia (ASIA — Korean women)
- Married woman: My Wife (SOAV — infidelity scenarios), Go Go's Black (GBSA)
- Amateur/public: Broccoli (BLOR), JPS (JPSB)
- Fetish: Fetish Box (ATFB — soapland/sex work roleplay), Katsuo Products (KATU — plus-size women)
**Art Video (historical note):** Japan's first SM-specialty video maker. Founded 1982 by Kawamura Yoshio (pseudonym Mine Kazuya, 1950–2017). First production: 地下室の淫魔 ("The Succubus in the Underground"). Sold exclusively via mail order through SM magazine ads. Incorporated 1983 for NEVA membership. Joined Mousouzoku BLACK LABEL 2010. Ceased new production ~2015 (remaster-only). Founder died April 2017; website closed June 30, 2017. Mousouzoku created Art Video SM (ADVO) as successor label.
**Notes:** The Emmanuel Group absorption (October 2014) is notable — the database still lists WILL/Emmanuel Group separately, suggesting internal organizational divisions persist within the umbrella. KUKI Inc. was also part of this network through shared NEVA membership and X CITY distribution before dissolution in 2017.
