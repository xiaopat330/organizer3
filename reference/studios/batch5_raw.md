# Studio Research — Batch 5: Independents + Uncensored Studios

## Cinemagic
**Founded:** November 11, 1983 (one of the oldest AV studios in Japan)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Was affiliated with Soft On Demand's Contents Soft Association (CSA) before CSA closure; currently independent
**Specialty:** Bondage, S&M, and simulated rape content produced with cinematic craft — elaborate set design, period costumes, and narrative frameworks (film noir detective stories, fantasy kingdoms, ninja/samurai settings). The "Cinemagic" name reflects the studio's ambition to apply genuine filmmaking artistry to SM content. Among the highest production values in the SM niche. 8 active sub-labels each targeting specific variations (Eva, Gang, kanjuku, NOIR, Vixen, Jyou, Lilies, Collect).
**Notable actresses:** Eri Kikuchi (debut September 1985 — CineMagic's first major star), Nozomi Momoi, Maria Yumeno
**Notable series:** Immoral Angel, Slave Secretary, The Big Breast Slave Girl, Tortured Race Queen
**Notes:** Founded November 11, 1983 — among the very first AV companies, pre-dating the VCR era's mainstream penetration. Located in Maruyama, Nakano-ku, Tokyo (25 employees). Over 1,000 titles produced across 40+ years. Early star Eri Kikuchi debuted in 1985 with "Beautiful D-Cup Girl, Sister L."
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CineMagic_Co.

## Glory Quest
**Founded:** At least June 2000 (first release Sweet Doll 01, June 10, 2000 on VHS)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent (GQE Inc.; incorporated ~2010 with 3M yen capital; CEO Ken Miyasaka; Toshima, Tokyo)
**Specialty:** Wide-spectrum fetish video producer. Core output spans big-bust (巨乳) content, elaborate scenario dramas, and mature woman genres. The "Maniac" sub-label covers extreme niches including fisting, S&M, and other hardcore fetishes. "Elder porn" featuring Shigeo Tokuda (male star born 1934) became a globally noted curiosity — covered by international media.
**Notable actresses:** Hotaru Akane, Yumi Kazama, Momoka Nishina
**Notable performers:** Shigeo Tokuda (male elder star, born 1934)
**Notable series:** Busty Medical Certificate, Forbidden Nursing, Incest, She Male Jam; Maniac label (extreme content)
**Awards:** 2009 AV Grand Prix Supporters Award for "Incest Family"
**Notes:** By December 2019, DMM listed 3,532 Glory Quest videos. ~18–20 releases/month. Independent operation — not part of WILL, SOD, or Prestige conglomerates. The elder star Shigeo Tokuda received significant international press coverage.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glory_Quest

## Maxing
**Founded:** 2006 (first videos released October 16, 2006)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent (Shibuya, Tokyo)
**Specialty:** Premium-tier independent label specializing in videos for sale (not rental). Approximately 15 new releases per month. Multi-label strategy: Bodyva, Cool Sexy, MAXING 3D, Natural Beauty, Pretty Cute, Roppongi. Known for attracting established talent from major studios — roster has included some of the industry's biggest names. Among the more prestigious independent labels.
**Notable actresses:** Akiho Yoshizawa, Naho Ozawa, Mihiro, Elly Akira, Maria Yumeno, Nao Oikawa, Tsubomi, Yuzuka Kinoshita
**Notable series:** Fu-Zoku Channel (風俗ちゃんねる), multi-label releases
**Awards:** 2010 Adult Broadcasting Awards Best Program (for Yuzuka Kinoshita debut); participated in 2007 AV Open and 2008 AV Grand Prix
**Notes:** First videos October 16, 2006. Expanded to Blu-ray in 2008, 3D format in 2010. Based in Shibuya. The "guitar labels" designation in the source data refers to the independent distribution channel these studios used historically.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maxing

## Venus (Crystal Eizou)
**Founded:** Crystal-Eizou founded October 1984, incorporated June 1985; Venus label launched June 1997
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Crystal Eizou / Crystal-Eizou (クリスタル映像) — independent
**Specialty:** Single actress featured videos and big-bust (巨乳) genre productions. Crystal-Eizou is one of the veteran independent studios. The Venus label (launched 1997) is their premium flagship. Known for elegant, cinematically polished solo actress showcases.
**Notable actresses:** Ai Iijima (debut 1992, considered "top AV actress of her era"), Kaoru Kuroki (debut October 1986), Yumika Hayashi
**Notable series:** Venus label (premium), The Costume Play Maid
**Awards:** 2003 Takeshi Kitano Awards Best AV Title for "Battle Without Foreplay?"; 2007 AV Open 2nd place for "Celebrity Debut - Misa Kikoden"
**Notes:** Crystal-Eizou is one of the oldest independent AV companies, predating SOD's founding by over a decade. President Tadaharu Nishimura. Located in Toshima, Tokyo. Ai Iijima was one of the industry's most famous crossover celebrities in the 1990s.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crystal-Eizou

## Tokyo Hot
**Founded:** Early 2000s (one of the original uncensored streaming platforms)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent (servers historically operated outside Japan to circumvent obscenity laws)
**Specialty:** Uncensored (無修正, mushūsei) streaming production. One of the pioneering uncensored AV platforms. Two-tier content: "n" series (n0000 codes) for professional AV actresses, "k" series (k0000 codes) for amateur/public content. Known for hardcore uncensored scenarios. Operating internationally allows circumvention of Japanese obscenity law mosaic requirements.
**Notable series:** n-series (professional, n0000 format), k-series (amateur, k0000 format)
**Notes:** Among the earliest and most established uncensored JAV platforms. The dual n/k coding system is a distinctive identifier. International server operation is the key to the no-mosaic production model.
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data; Tokyo-Hot Wikidata

## Heyzo
**Founded:** c. 2011
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** DTI Services group (alongside 1Pondo and Caribbeancom)
**Specialty:** Uncensored (無修正) streaming platform at the high-quality end of the uncensored market. Compared to most uncensored sites, Heyzo production quality is closer to mainstream AV standards — better lighting, more narrative, higher-production-value shoots. Regularly features known censored AV actresses making uncensored appearances. HEYZO-XXXX 4-digit code format.
**Notable series:** HEYZO (single unified code, HEYZO-XXXX format)
**Notes:** Part of the DTI Services group that also runs 1Pondo and Caribbeancom — a consolidated uncensored content network. The DTI group is one of the most recognized in uncensored JAV. Heyzo occupies the "premium uncensored" niche, bridging the gap between mainstream censored AV and the more rough-and-ready typical uncensored output.
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data; ZoomInfo Heyzo

## GIGA
**Founded:** c. 1995 (based in Minato, Tokyo)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent (GIGA Co., Ltd.)
**Specialty:** Tokusatsu/superhero heroine AV — the definitive label for this unique niche. Actresses portray costumed superheroines (Super Sentai-style, magical girls, Kamen Rider-adjacent characters) who are captured, defeated, and violated in scenarios. Blends Japanese tokusatsu special effects culture with AV in a way no other studio does. Substantial global cult following. In 2021, GIGA launched a YouTube channel (GIGA Tokusatsu Channel) producing all-ages tokusatsu content — adult-content DVDs are sold separately.
**Notable series:** GIGL (main label), various tokusatsu series including Kyuusei Sentai Wakuseiber, Kaiju Sentai Jyuukaizar, Shoudo Sentai Goukengo
**Notes:** Based in Minato, Tokyo (~1995). The YouTube strategy (all-ages content on public channels + adult content on paid DVD/digital) is a novel distribution approach. The studio has become a recognized enough cultural curiosity that international media has covered the tokusatsu heroine genre.
**Sources:** GIGA Tokusatsu Fandom wiki; aniSearch GIGA Co., Ltd.; search results

## Center Village
**Founded:** Unknown (veteran independent, active 2000s–present)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent
**Specialty:** Mature woman specialist at the older end of the age spectrum. Content typically features women in their 40s through 60s, distinct from mainstream jukujo labels that typically focus on women in their 30s-40s. Smaller boutique operation compared to major mature labels (Madonna, Tameike Goro) but with a dedicated audience seeking more distinctly mature performers.
**Notable series:** HTDT (single unified code)
**Notes:** One of several independent jukujo specialists. Compared to Crystal-Eizou and Ruby (other independents in this space), Center Village occupies the older-focus end. Limited public documentation on founding history.
**Sources:** Amazon product listings; internal labels.yaml data
