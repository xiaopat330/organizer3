# Studio Research — Batch 4: SOD Subsidiaries + Prestige + KMP

## ROCKET (SOD)
**Founded:** October 2007 (as SOD group affiliate)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD) group
**Specialty:** Comedy, absurdist, and fantasy-concept planning content. "Delivering unique ideas and works not seen elsewhere." Famous for outrageous premises: time-stop scenarios, aphrodisiac experiments, taboo step-family dynamics, game show formats with sexual consequences, increasingly bizarre/creative setups. One of the most prolific planning labels in AV — the RCT series reached 970+ titles as of early 2026. Lighter/more humorous tone than most planning labels.
**Notable series:** RCT (main series, 970+ titles), RCTD (specialty planning)
**Notes:** Founded October 2007 as SOD affiliate. In-house scripting team develops concepts emphasizing originality. RCT is one of the most recognized single product codes in the industry by sheer volume and brand recognition. The studio's comedic/absurdist approach distinguishes it from serious-toned competitors.
**Sources:** Grokipedia RCT JAV series; NamuWiki Rocket entry

## SWITCH (SOD)
**Founded:** Unknown (SOD subsidiary, active 2010s)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD) group
**Specialty:** Harem-style scenario planning content. Best known for the "I'm the only man in [setting]" format — a single male protagonist surrounded by multiple women (house, dormitory, office, etc.). Single code SW for all releases. Simple, high-fantasy premise executed at high volume.
**Notable series:** SW (single unified code)
**Notes:** Part of the SOD subsidiary ecosystem. The harem format has proven consistently popular. SOD subsidiary with moderate release volume.

## Prestige
**Founded:** February 2002
**Status:** Active (independent — departed DMM/FANZA March 2021, now exclusive to MGStage)
**Parent:** Independent (not affiliated with WILL, SOD, or Hokuto)
**Specialty:** One of Japan's "Big 3" AV conglomerates alongside WILL and SOD. Distinctive for rapid label cycling — over 200 product codes have existed, most short-lived (months to ~2 years). Known for: (1) amateur hunting/street pickup series (iconic EDD/ESK and related codes); (2) the ABP/ABW/ABF exclusive actress flagship line; (3) the MGS Video download platform, which Prestige pioneered and which includes Shiroto TV, Luxu TV, and other channels. The hunting/pickup series were among the most commercially successful content in the 2000s–2010s.
**Notable actresses (ABP/ABW/ABF line):** Ayu Sakurai, Rui Saotome, Mana Sakura, many others across 20+ years
**Notable series:** EDD/ESK (escort/amateur hunting); ABP→ABW→ABF (exclusive actress main series); MGS download channel series; KACHO (celebrity crossover, imitator of MUTEKI)
**Awards:** Participated in major industry awards; Rui Saotome's DOM-001 noted as particularly famous release
**Notes:** Founded February 2002. By March 2021 DMM listed over 13,000 titles. In March 2021, Prestige departed DMM/FANZA — a major industry event — and moved exclusively to MGStage platform. The label cycling strategy (200+ codes) allowed testing many niches simultaneously; successful concepts were extended, unsuccessful ones quietly retired.
**Sources:** Everybodywiki Prestige entry; Wikidata PRESTIGE; NamuWiki Prestige entry

## KMP (K.M. Produce)
**Founded:** April 2002 (became independent; took over Million label from DMN/Digital Media Network)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent (not affiliated with WILL, SOD, or Prestige)
**Specialty:** Major independent AV company. The Million label (MILD/MILV/MKMP codes) is the flagship, running since 2002. Also operates Scoop (amateur content/SCOP) and S-Class Amateur (SAMA). In 2011 absorbed the formerly separate Media Station and Real Works labels. One of the larger independents outside the WILL/SOD/Prestige "Big 3," offering solid mid-tier production across genres. Based in Shibuya, Tokyo.
**Notable actresses:** Various across the Million label's 20+ year history
**Notable series:** Million (MILD/MILV/MKMP), Okazu (OKAD), Scoop (SCOP), S-Class Amateur (SAMA), EC/Ecstasy (harder content)
**Notes:** The Million label was originally used by DMN (Digital Media Network) before KMP took it over in April 2002. DMN's website closed November 2002. KMP operated websites for Media Station and Real Works in addition to its own before absorbing both in February 2011. The Million brand is among the most recognizable in mid-tier JAV.
**Sources:** KM Produce Wikipedia (en.wikipediam.org); search results
