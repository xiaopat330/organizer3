# Studio Research — Batch 2: WILL Direct Labels (non-flagship)

## Madonna
**Founded:** 2003 (website October 2003; first video JUC-001 December 2003; company incorporated 2002)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** Japan's premier jukujo (熟女, mature woman/MILF) label. Effectively "the S1 of the mature genre" — same parent company as S1 but focused entirely on married women and mature actresses. Enormous volume (~20 original and compilation releases/month). In-house sub-labels for progressively older women (Obasan for 40s, Monroe for 50s+).
**Notable actresses:** Yumi Kazama, Kyoko Kazama, Yumika Hayashi, Eri Kikuchi, Maki Tomoda, Aki Tomosaki
**Notable series:** Sex at 40, Sex at 50, Friend's Mother, My Aunt, Apartment Wife, Narekomi manga adaptation series (URE)
**Notes:** By 2011 DMM listed 2,082 Madonna DVDs. Launched Fitch sub-label (busty content) November 2007. First 3D video February 2011. 2009 AV Grand Prix Special Award for Mature Woman Videos (for "Raped Female Teacher").
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madonna_(studio)

## kawaii*
**Founded:** c. 2014 (inferred from KAWD code series inception)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd.
**Specialty:** Cute, youthful-looking actress specialist. The aesthetic is "adorable" (かわいい) — petite, baby-faced, innocent-seeming women. KAWD ran to KAWD-999 before transitioning to CAWD in 2020. One of the fastest-growing WILL labels in the 2010s.
**Notable actresses:** Various debut/rookie actresses; label is known for launching new careers rather than established exclusives
**Notable series:** KAWD (2014–2020), CAWD (2020–present), planning series (KAPD), best compilations (KWBD)
**Notes:** The asterisk is part of the official brand name. Sister label to Candy (another debut-focused WILL label).
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data; KAWD/CAWD code structure

## Kira Kira
**Founded:** c. 2007–2010 (inferred from BLK/KIRD code structure)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd.
**Specialty:** Gyaru (ギャル) specialist — tanned, bronzed, deliberately garish/flashy aesthetic typical of the gyaru subculture. "Kira Kira" (キラキラ) = "sparkling/glittering." BLK (Black Gyaru) is the primary code. Content reflects the gyaru lifestyle aesthetic — flashy fashion, tanned skin, bold makeup.
**Notable actresses:** Various gyaru-aesthetic exclusives
**Notable series:** BLK (Kirakira Black Gyaru), SET (Street Gyaru), KIFD (festival/event releases)
**Notes:** Represents one of the most distinctly Japanese subculture niches in AV — gyaru culture was at peak popularity in the 2000s–2010s. The label captures the aesthetic authentically.
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data

## E-BODY
**Founded:** c. 2007 (inferred from EBOD code series; confirmed as Hokuto/WILL internal label)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** Voluptuous body-type specialist. "E-body" = エロボディ (ero-body = sexy/erotic body). Focuses on actresses with large breasts, curves, athletic builds, and strong physical presence. Overlaps with Fitch and OPPAI in the busty niche but distinguishes itself by emphasizing the full-body ideal — not just breast size but overall glamorous physique.
**Notable actresses:** Various voluptuous exclusives; also Momose Yurina (Hana Tsuma/EYAN married woman sub-series)
**Notable series:** EBOD (main), EBWH (main successor), EYAN (Hana Tsuma married woman sub-series), EBVR (VR)
**Notes:** MKCK is the best-of compilation code. One of several WILL labels in the busty/glamour space alongside OPPAI, Fitch, and PREMIUM.
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data; E-BODY Wikipedia (ja)

## PREMIUM
**Founded:** c. 2010 (inferred from PGD code inception)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd.
**Specialty:** Elegant, sophisticated idol-class content at the premium tier. Emphasis on glamour, class, and refined aesthetics. Actresses are selected for polished appearance and "premium" quality. The label occupies the space between S1's aspirational glamour and PREMIUM's more understated elegance. PGD (Glamorous, older) → PRED (Elegance, current).
**Notable actresses:** Various high-end exclusives
**Notable series:** PGD (Glamorous, former), PRED (Elegance, current flagship), PID (Image), PJD (Jewel), PXD (Xanadu specialty)
**Notes:** One of the smaller but consistently high-quality WILL idol labels.
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data

## Tameike Goro
**Founded:** February 2006 (independent company); label MDYD began December 2003 as a Moodyz sub-series
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation; incorporated as own company under Hokuto umbrella)
**Specialty:** Mature woman (熟女, jukujo) content directed by Goro Tameike, widely considered the pioneer of the modern jukujo genre. Tameike began directing in 1994 (Max-A Samansa label), spent years at SOD, then launched MDYD at Moodyz (December 2003). Became independent in February 2006. Distinctive auteur approach — Tameike's philosophy is that a "mature woman has become beautiful through her life experiences."
**Notable actresses:** Mariko Kawana (his wife; debut video 1999 under SOD), Nao Oikawa, Hitomi Hayasaka, Sayuri Shiraishi, Ayane Asakura
**Notable series:** MDYD (Moodyz-era series), MEYD (flagship after independence)
**Awards:** 2004 KMP Million Academy Awards (multiple); 2006 Moodyz Awards Best Director (3rd); 2008/2009 AV Grand Prix Mature Woman Video awards; 2014 AV Open (Middleweight 2nd)
**Notes:** The MDYD series was originally a Moodyz label directed by Tameike before he became independent. Goro Tameike graduated from Meiji University Law School before entering the AV directing industry.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goro_Tameike

## Fitch
**Founded:** November 2007 (launched as Madonna sub-label)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (sub-label under Madonna)
**Specialty:** Busty (巨乳, bakunyū) actress specialist. Spun off from Madonna in 2007 to separately serve the large-breast content market. While Madonna focuses on the mature woman genre, Fitch targets the busty actress niche across age groups. JUFD→JUFE code progression.
**Notable actresses:** Various busty exclusives
**Notable series:** JUFD (original busty code, old), JUFE (current busty code), FINH (Namadore amateur), JUNY (Nikuyoku/carnal desire series)
**Notes:** Launched from within Madonna in November 2007 as bakunyū specialist. Closely related to OPPAI and E-BODY in the WILL busty space.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madonna_(studio)

## BeFree
**Founded:** Unknown (active c. 2010s)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd.
**Specialty:** Workplace fantasy scenarios featuring beautiful women in professional roles — office ladies, nurses, teachers, flight attendants. The "BeFree" name suggests liberation/freedom. All-purpose beautiful-woman-in-uniform content. BF is one of the simpler product codes in the WILL lineup (all releases use just "BF").
**Notable actresses:** Various WILL-roster actresses in workplace scenarios
**Notable series:** BF (single unified code for all releases)
**Notes:** Content is scenario-driven — the appeal is the professional setting and uniform as much as the performer.
**Sources:** Internal labels.yaml data

## Attackers
**Founded:** 1996 (first videos February 1997 on Shark label)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation; originally independent)
**Specialty:** Drama-driven SM, NTR, and rape-fantasy content. WILL's oldest narrative drama label. One of the longest-running labels in the industry. Created IDEA POCKET as its "Angel" cosplay sub-label in December 1998; IDEA POCKET later became independent. ADN (Adult Drama/NTR) is the most commercially dominant code, widely considered the gold standard for NTR drama in the industry.
**Notable actresses:** Sally Yoshino, Kyoko Kazama, Yuri Komuro (early 1990s–2000s)
**Notable series:** ADN (NTR drama — most popular series), Jabaku/JBD (tight-bondage SM), Ryubaku/RBD-RBK (restraint SM), Shark/SHKD (refined SM), Slave Island (2005–2008), Les Noir (lesbian SM 2005–2007)
**Awards:** "Slave Island" won Attackers Award at 2005 Moodyz Awards; participated in AV Grand Prix 2008–2009
**Notes:** By 2011 DMM listed 1,500+ Attackers titles. The Shark label released first four videos February 1997. Spawned IDEA POCKET as its "Angel" imprint in December 1998 — IDEA POCKET became one of the WILL Big 3. ADN code consistently ranks among the most-purchased drama codes industrywide.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attackers
