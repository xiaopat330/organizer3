# Studio Research — Batch 3: SOD Group + WILL Subcontractors

## Das! (WILL/Hokuto)
**Founded:** 2007 (first videos April 25, 2007)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** Hardcore scenario content — simulated rape, gangbang, bukake, nakadashi (creampie), and humiliation scenarios using well-known actress talent. The name "Das!" appears to be a German-inspired exclamatory branding choice. Distinctly more aggressive than WILL's standard labels. Platinum sub-label (softer content) launched March 2010.
**Notable actresses:** Maria Ozawa, Rin Aoki, Bunko Kanazawa, Hitomi Tanaka, Asami Sugiura
**Notable series:** DASD (original flagship), DASS (current flagship), DAZD (best compilation), PLA (Platinum — softer content)
**Awards:** "The QUEEN of DAS!" won Best Violence Video Award at 2009 AV Grand Prix
**Notes:** Releases ~4 original and compilation videos per month. Distributes via DMM in DVD and Blu-ray. Originally started as Hokuto client company; now WILL subcontractor.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Das_(studio)

## dogma (WILL subcontractor)
**Founded:** February 2001 (as SOD group label, separated ~2002 as independent "indie" producer)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Currently: WILL Co., Ltd. (Hokuto Corporation) — distributed via DMM. Originally: Soft On Demand (SOD) group under director Tohjiro
**Specialty:** Extreme fetish content — the most hardcore label in the WILL stable. Tohjiro's label specializes in extreme BDSM, anal, bondage, S&M, forced fellatio, enemas, watersports, and scat. Director-driven auteur studio with TOHJIRO as its central creative force. Gold Tojiro (GTJ) sub-series is known for historically-accurate punishment scenes and whip usage. Corporate name: OM Production, Inc.
**Notable directors:** TOHJIRO (founder, most prolific), Hitoshi Nimura, Be-Bop Minoru
**Notable actresses:** Kurumi Morishita (contract, retired 2009), Mayura Hoshitsuki (contract, retired 2010)
**Notable series:** GTJ (Gold Tojiro — historical punishment/whipping), DDT (regular), Confinement Chair Trance (from November 2002), M-Drug S&M series (from September 2005), Innocent Desire (from March 2001)
**Awards:** Tohjiro won D-1 Climax Awards 2005 and 2006; Dogma won 2009 AV GrandPrix Digital Sales Award
**Notes:** Started within SOD under director Tohjiro in February 2001. Left SOD ~2002 to become independent "indie" producer. Headquarters in Shinjuku, Tokyo. Currently distributes via Hokuto/WILL's DMM platform. Among the most extreme mainstream AV labels.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dogma_(studio)

## FALENO (SOD group)
**Founded:** December 2018 (incorporated); first releases February 2019
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD) group
**Specialty:** Next-generation streaming-first premium label. Designed to compete directly with S1 No.1 Style and IDEA POCKET for top-tier exclusive actresses. Streaming-first distribution model with unlimited viewing membership. Aggressively recruited exclusives from other major studios. Made immediate industry impact by attracting high-profile names from WILL and other studios.
**Notable actresses:** Arina Hashimoto, Yuko Ono, Nene Yoshitaka, Moe Amatsuka, Harukaze Hikaru
**Notable series:** FSDSS (Faleno Star — main exclusive actress releases), FSVSS (VR), FCDSS (8-hour compilations), FLNO/FLNS (advance/pre-release)
**Notes:** Founded December 2018, distribution began February 2019. Three debut actresses released in first three months. Streaming-first model (unlimited membership access) was a significant departure from traditional physical media distribution. Part of SOD group.
**Sources:** NamuWiki FALENO entry; search results

## SOD Create
**Founded:** March 1999 (as "Hamlet" production unit; renamed SOD Create 2001)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD) — primary production division
**Specialty:** SOD's core production unit and the creative engine of the entire SOD group. Famous for innovative, outrageous concept releases: Magic Mirror van series (SDMM — one-way mirror van street pickups), female employee series (SDJS — SOD's own employees in AV), CFNM (clothed female naked male) "penis study" videos, hypnosis scenarios, quiz show formats. SOD Star (STAR/STARS/START code) is the exclusive actress flagship — direct competitor to S1 and IDEA POCKET.
**Notable actresses (SOD Star):** Aoi Kururugi (current), Noa Fujikawa, Nene Tanaka, Rena Aoi, Yua Shirasaki (former exclusive); many others in SOD's vast history
**Notable series:** SDMM (Magic Mirror), SDJS (Female Employees), SDNM (Real Married Women), SDDE (Senz project), SOD Star (STARS/START)
**Notes:** SOD was founded December 1995 by Ganari Takahashi. SOD Create (née Hamlet) established March 1999. SOD grew from ¥300M in sales (1996) to ¥9B (2005). SOD Star directly competes with S1 and IDEA POCKET for the top exclusive actress market. SOD also runs its own streaming platform (SOD Prime).
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soft_On_Demand

## Silk Labo (SOD)
**Founded:** 2009 (launched by SOD as josei-muke/women's AV label)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD)
**Specialty:** Female-oriented/women's AV. One of the very few commercially successful "AV for women" labels in the industry. Content is romance-focused, softcore-leaning, emphasizing emotional narrative and handsome male actors. Male "eromen" (エロメン) actors have organized fan clubs and hold fan events (handshake events, photo sessions, "lunch dates" for premium members). Ittetsu Suzuki is the signature male actor.
**Notable (male) performers:** Ittetsu Suzuki (defining face of the label)
**Notable series:** SILK (single flagship code)
**Notes:** Founded 2009 after SOD observed female fans of male performer Ittetsu Suzuki. The male eromen fandom culture around Silk Labo was academically studied as a form of commodified intimacy. Represents a genuine attempt to address the female consumer in the JAV market — one of the few that succeeded commercially.
**Sources:** Tokyo Reporter, academic research on Silk Labo; Soft On Demand Wikipedia

## Dandy (SOD)
**Founded:** February 2006 (founder Dandy Yoshino left Natural High to create it)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD) group
**Specialty:** Scenario-driven planning content — primarily male-POV fantasy situations. Elaborate setups covering fetish scenarios, costume play, group/harem situations, NTR, workplace fantasy, and increasingly creative premises. One of SOD's highest-volume planning labels. Direct rival to Hunter (WILL/Triple H Group).
**Notable series:** DANDY (single unified code for all releases)
**Notes:** Dandy Yoshino founded the label in February 2006 after leaving Natural High (also SOD group). The DANDY rivalry with Hunter (WILL) is one of the defining label competitions in the planning genre. Very high release volume.
**Sources:** DANDY Wikipedia (ja); Soft On Demand Wikipedia

## Natural High (SOD)
**Founded:** 1999 (joined SOD group August 1999)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Soft On Demand (SOD) group
**Specialty:** Molestation (chikan, 痴漢) and voyeur scenario specialist. One of the longest-running labels in this specific niche. Content primarily features public transportation and crowd-based molestation scenarios. Known for some releases with very unusual/unique concept twists within the chikan genre.
**Notable actresses:** Elly Akira (associated with Natural High/Dogma)
**Notable series:** NHDT (original code), NHDTA/NHDTB/NHDTC (current codes), NIC (newer umbrella code)
**Notes:** Established 1999, joined SOD group August 1999. DANDY founder Dandy Yoshino previously worked at Natural High before leaving in February 2006.
**Sources:** Soft On Demand Wikipedia; NamuWiki Natural High entry
