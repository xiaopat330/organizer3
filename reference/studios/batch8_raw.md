## M's Video Group (エムズビデオグループ / MVG)
**Founded:** ~1996 (original); revived December 2005 under director Yamada Chin (ヤマダ団地)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (Outvision distribution)
**Specialty:** Semen-swallowing (gokkun / ごっくん) and creampie content. Studio tagline: "ザーメン・ゴックンの殿堂" ("Temple of Semen and Swallowing"). Despite the word "group" in the name, it is a single-label operation. MVSD (standard), MVBD (best), MVGD (general), MVMD (mania/fetish).
**Origin story:** Founded by AV director Matsumoto Kazuhiko, who operated a direct-retail store "Milky Shop M's" in Shibuya's Dogenzaka with a strict "fans only" door policy — visitors were quizzed on their AV knowledge before entry. Original Ichigo Ichie (一期一会, 1996) series sold 200,000+ copies. Company revived December 2005 with Outvision distribution.
**Notable actresses:** Akari Neo (exclusive January 2022 – August 2025)
**Notable series:** Ichigo Ichie (1996, 200,000+ copies), Housewife Home Salon (2022–present), First and Best Anal Release (2016–2021, 2024), Wealthy Old Man's Bulk Creampie Purchase (2016–2025)
**Notes:** Titles release on the third Tuesday of each month. M's Awards presented at MOODYZ group year-end events.
**Sources:** Wikipedia ja エムズビデオグループ

## Momotaro Video Publishing (桃太郎映像出版)
**Founded:** January 19, 1995 (incorporated as stock company)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (Outvision subcontractor); rental distribution via T.I.S. / Momotaro Pictures division (est. 2005)
**Headquarters:** Bunkyo Ward, Tokyo. Capital ¥10M; rep. Asano Kuniichi; ~30 employees
**Specialty:** General multi-genre content — amateur, roleplay/cosplay, creampie, soapland fantasy, international co-productions. One of the few Japanese indie AV producers to maintain direct international talent networks (up to 6 months negotiation per foreign performer). ALD (primary), KED (secondary), YMDD (secondary).
**Notable actresses:** nao., Yuuki Rin, Sato Erika, Ninomiya Saki
**Notable series:** B★Jean, Wild Thing, SOAP, 乳魂 (Breast Soul), Splash Girl, 酔娘伝 (Drunken Chronicles), Yariман Wagon
**Subsidiary:** 素人onlyプラム (Amateur-only Plum)
**Scale:** ~¥1.6 billion sales (FY November 2010); ranked 3rd in sales among independent AV producers at peak
**Name:** Momotarō (桃太郎) = Japan's most famous folk hero "Peach Boy" — chosen for brand recognition and memorability
**Sources:** Wikipedia ja 桃太郎 (アダルトビデオ); Weblio 桃太郎映像出版

## Deep's (ディープス)
**Founded:** August 1999 (as SOD group label)
**Legal name:** 株式会社東凛 (Torin Co., Ltd.)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Originally SOD (Soft On Demand); departed SOD October 2016; now WILL Co., Ltd. (Outvision)
**Headquarters:** 2-15-26 Shinjuku, Shinjuku Ward, Tokyo. Capital ¥30M; ~25 employees
**Specialty:** Variety/planning (kikaku) with wide genre range. Internally tiered: Level 1 = foreign talent, general public, lesbian; Level 2 = street/homeless or planning-based; Level 3 = weakly anal to hard fetish. Most famous as the creator of the **Magic Mirror Van** (マジックミラー号) concept — one of the most recognized series in all of Japanese AV. After SOD split, rebranded as "The Magic Mirror" (ザ・マジックミラー). DVDES (primary), DVUES, DVUMA, DVDMS (monitoring/documentary).
**Notable directors:** Mamezou (founder/principal), Greenhouse Effect (温室効果), Funky Hasei, Nagase Swing
**Notable series:** Magic Mirror Van (25th anniversary ~2022); Magic Mirror Delivery; General Men and Women Monitoring AV; Homeless series (starring Tsubomi, Nozomi Hazuki, Rei Mizuna)
**Awards:** AV Grand Prix 2008 (multiple); AV Open 2014–2015
**Revenue:** ~¥900M (FY June 2017)
**Notes:** The October 2016 departure from SOD (alongside Hunter) was a significant industry event — the Magic Mirror series had been considered a core SOD property. Its migration to WILL was one of the larger corporate moves of that year.
**Sources:** Wikipedia ja ディープス; Deep's corporate page (deeps.co.jp); Soft On Demand Wikipedia (en)

## Marx (マルクス興業)
**Founded:** Unknown
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (Outvision subcontractor)
**Specialty:** Comprehensive maker — projects, dramas, POV/subjective viewpoints, and documentaries. Particularly strong in married woman (人妻/hitozuma) content. Also produces hardcore and maniac works. SMA (standard), SMS (pregnant women / 孕ませ content), SBB (best compilations).
**Name:** マルクス興業 = "Marx Industries/Entertainment." The 興業 suffix is common for Japanese entertainment companies. "Marx" is almost certainly a reference to the Marx Brothers comedy troupe — Western pop culture naming was common in 1990s-2000s Japanese AV. No dedicated Wikipedia page found.
**Sources:** NamuWiki AV label reference (WILL subcontractor section); reference/namu_wiki_av_labels.md
