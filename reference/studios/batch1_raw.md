# Studio Research — Batch 1: WILL Big 3 + WANZ + MUTEKI

## Moodyz
**Founded:** 2000 (website March 2000; first release September 2000 — "Costume Princess" starring Maiko Yuki)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** High-volume, high-production flagship label. Enormous exclusive actress roster. Covers essentially every genre — drama, fetish, gyaru, mature, hardcore — through its extensive sub-label system. One of the absolute highest release volumes in the industry.
**Notable actresses:** An Nanba (exclusive 2002–2009, 3-time Moodyz Award winner), Bunko Kanazawa (early star, 2000), Ai Uehara (major exclusive ~2012–2017), Saki Okuda, Shoko Takahashi (long-running exclusive), Mia Nanasawa, Tsubomi, Jinguji Nao, Rena Miyashita
**Notable series:** Dream School (ドリーム学園, launched July 2001 — signature blockbuster series, multiple AV Open placements), Killer Label Dream Woman series, Moody's Acid (Kikatan specialty line)
**Notes:** By September 2011 had over 4,000 DVD titles and 1,200 VHS tapes. One of Japan's highest-volume studios. Dream School 2 and 5 won Moodyz Award Grand Prize (2001–2002). Dream School 10 placed 2nd at AV Open 2006, Dream School 11 placed 3rd at AV Open 2007. Runs sub-labels for almost every niche.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moodyz

## S1 No.1 Style
**Founded:** 2004 (website June 30, 2004; first releases November 2004)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** WILL's premium flagship label for top-tier idol-class exclusive actresses. Highest production values in the industry. Pioneered Blu-ray (September 2009) and 3D format (June 2010) releases in the JAV space. "Girigiri" reduced-mosaic technology introduced late 2004. Known for attracting the industry's biggest names.
**Notable actresses:** Sora Aoi (founding actress, November 2004), Akiho Yoshizawa (2007 Vegas Night top), Yuma Asami, Tsukasa Aoi, Yua Mikami (joined 2016, ex-SKE48), Eimi Fukada, Yu Shinoda
**Notable series:** First 11 releases launched simultaneously November 2004. S1 Premium Box series. Extensive solo actress series for each exclusive.
**Notes:** Produced over 5,100 movies in 21 years. First place at AV Open 2006 and 2007. 2009 AV GrandPrix top award. 2008 AV Grand Prix Grand Prix Stage Highest Award. For 5th anniversary (2010) organized an All Japan Pro Wrestling event — unusual cross-promotional stunt. Headquarters in Meguro, Tokyo. Widely considered the prestige top-tier label in the industry.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S1_No._1_Style

## IDEA POCKET
**Founded:** 2002 (website June 19, 2002; roots as "Angel" cosplay sub-label under Attackers from December 1998)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** Beautiful idol-class actresses with emphasis on high-end cinematography and storytelling. Compared to S1's more aspirational glamour, IDEA POCKET leans into a warmer, more romantic/intimate aesthetic. Releases approximately 17–18 videos monthly. One of the "Big 3" WILL labels alongside Moodyz and S1.
**Notable actresses:** Aino Kishi, Rio, Ai Haneda, Jessica Kizaki, Rina Ishihara, Tsumugi Akari, Kana Momonogi, Momo Sakura, Karen Kaede, Airi Kijima
**Notable series:** First Impression (debut series), Angel series (legacy), Let's Do It at School!, Max Mosaic, Spermania, Kiss and Sex
**Notes:** Originally the "Angel" label under Attackers (first video AN-001 December 1998 starring Ryo Hitomi), spun out as independent IDEA POCKET in 2002. Over 3,500 titles produced. Released first 3D video in 2011. Partnered with Union Pro Wrestling in 2013.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IdeaPocket

## WANZ Factory
**Founded:** 2012 (per label codes — WANZ series begins 2012; note: unrelated Wikipedia article may describe a different entity)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd.
**Specialty:** Creative planning and fantasy/scenario-driven content. Fetish concepts, workplace scenarios, and role-play with WILL-caliber production quality. Known for witty, concept-forward titles. Flagship code WANZ (2012–2020) succeeded by WAAA (2020–present).
**Notable actresses:** Shoko Takahashi (long-running exclusive, retired 2022), various WILL-roster actresses in planning roles
**Notable series:** Wands regular series (WANZ/WAAA), Best compilation (BMW), specialty planning releases (NWF)
**Notes:** Launched as a WILL subsidiary in 2012. Approximately 16–17 releases per month including planning originals. Inherits WILL's production values in a planning/concept-forward format. Shoko Takahashi was its most prominent exclusive before moving to Moodyz.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wanz_Factory (caution: may describe unrelated entity), labels.yaml internal data

## MUTEKI
**Founded:** 2008 (first release September 2008)
**Status:** Defunct/Inactive (final release TEK-098, February 2021)
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. (formerly Hokuto Corporation)
**Specialty:** Celebrity crossover label — exclusively casts mainstream entertainers (gravure idols, pop idols, mainstream actresses, singers) making their AV debut. Name means "invincible" (無敵). Each release was a major industry event given the talent involved.
**Notable actresses/personalities:** Yua Mikami (TEK-067, 2015 debut; became the most famous release in label history), Rika Hoshimi, Hana Haruna, Risa Naruse (Osaka Haruna, ex-AKB48), Momona Kitō (ex-SKE48), Yoko Shimada, Chiharu Komatsu, Azumi Uehara, Emiri Okazaki
**Notable releases:** TEK-067 (Yua Mikami debut, 2015 — among the highest-selling JAV releases ever), TEK-097 ("These Two Have No Equal" — Yua Mikami + Shoko Takahashi 10th anniversary collab, December 2018, 2nd highest-selling JAV of 2018)
**Notes:** Ran from 2008 to February 2021 (TEK-098 final release). Each new signing was a major news event in the industry. Yua Mikami's debut TEK-067 is considered one of the most commercially successful JAV releases of all time. The label's business model of recruiting idol/celebrity talent created a new sub-genre that other labels (Prestige's KACHO, etc.) have since imitated.
**Sources:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Muteki
