# Studio Research — Batch 7: Crystal Eizou + WILL Sub-Groups

## Crystal Eizou (クリスタル映像)
**Founded:** October 1984 (incorporated June 1985)
**Status:** Active
**Parent:** Independent
**Specialty:** Veteran independent jukujo (熟女) producer. CESD/CETD codes represent the studio's direct mature-woman productions; the premium Venus sub-label (VEC/VEMA) handles flagship solo-actress showcase content and is listed separately in the database.
**Notable actresses:** Ai Iijima (debut 1992, crossover celebrity of the 1990s); Kaoru Kuroki (debut October 1986); Yumika Hayashi
**Notes:** President Tadaharu Nishimura; Toshima, Tokyo. Won 2003 Takeshi Kitano Awards Best AV Title; 2007 AV Open 2nd place.
**Sources:** batch5_raw.md (Venus/Crystal-Eizou entry)

## WILL/Triple H Group (HHHグループ)
**Founded:** Hunter April 2007 (within SOD); group formed March 2016 when Hunter departed SOD
**Status:** Active (Hunter, OYC active; Apache ceased Sep 2021, AtoM ceased May 2021)
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. / Outvision distribution
**Name meaning:** HHH = "ホントにホットでエッチ" (Honto ni Hot de Ecchi = "Really Hot and Naughty") — a marketing acronym, not an abbreviation of label names
**Constituent labels:**
- **Hunter** (HUNTC): Founded April 2007 within SOD. "草食男子メーカー" (herbivore male maker) — fantasy scenarios for/about passive, non-assertive men. ~20 employees, Nakano Ward Tokyo. Trademark: extremely long titles (one 2015 release exceeded 400 characters). Director Borubo Nakano won two-crown at 2010 SOD Awards. Rivals Dandy (SOD) — defining competition in the kikaku planning genre.
- **Hunter Black** (HUBLK): Hunter's counterpart sub-label — "肉食男子メーカー" (carnivore male maker) covering more aggressive/dominant scenarios including lines and rape fantasy.
- **AtoM** (ATOM): Founded November 2010 within SOD. TV variety × AV — amateur-participatory game/penalty scenarios. ~181 titles total. Ceased May 2021.
- **Apache** (AP): Public/outdoor molestation scenarios (trains, buses, outdoors). Ceased September 2021.
- **Oyashoku Company / お夜食カンパニー** (OYC): Everyday monitoring-style scenarios. Active through at least 2025.
- **Green Apple / ぐりーんあっぷる** (GAPL): Daily project scenarios. Ceased 2019.
- **Golden Time** (GDHH): General planning. Ceased May 2021.
- **Paco Paco Dan** (PKPD): Active (confirmed social media).
- **Atlantis-H** (ATHH): Status unknown.
**Rivalry:** Hunter vs. Dandy is one of the defining label competitions in the kikaku genre. Both originated in SOD before Hunter departed in 2016, making the rivalry inter-group.
**Sources:** HHHグループ Wikipedia (ja); Hunter Wikipedia (ja); ATOM Wikipedia (ja); namu.wiki (Korean)

## WILL/Emmanuel Group (エマニエルグループ)
**Founded:** February 2009
**Status:** Active (most labels); some legacy codes inactive
**Parent:** WILL Co., Ltd. / Outvision (absorbed into Mousouzoku umbrella October 17, 2014, but continues operating as distinct sub-division)
**Name origin:** エマニエル = direct reference to *Emmanuelle* (1974 French erotic film starring Sylvia Kristel). In Japan the film grossed ~¥15.6 billion, achieved 30.8% TV ratings when first aired in 1977, and "エマニエル" became cultural shorthand for mature, sensual women's content — hence the brand name.
**What it is:** Distribution umbrella (not a single company) aggregating ~20+ sub-labels all targeting the jukujo (熟女) and hitozuma (人妻) space across progressively older age brackets. On FANZA listed as "メーカー名/エマニエル." Operates analogously to Mousouzoku but exclusively in the mature/elderly women niche.
**Age range structure:**
- 40s married women: EMAD (Jukudora), KITU, KSBJ, HBNK
- 40s–50s: EMEN (Enjuku), EMBW (Kasumi Kikaku, ~185 titles), EMBZ (Jukujo-juku/Ranbukan, 400+ titles), EMGB, ZUM
- 50s–60s: MRSS, EMTS, EMBX (Lady Baba), JKNK
- 50s–70s (extreme elder end): EMAZ (Fujinsha) — explicitly targets women in their 50s, 60s, and 70s
- SM with mature women: SIBA (Ranbukan) — mature woman SM specialty
- Amateur/public: TBKD, FLOW (Rōjin)
- General flagship: EMAF (Four Dimension) — 647+ titles, Feb 2009–May 2022, largest catalog in the group
**Notable sub-labels:**
- EMAF/Four Dimension: 647+ titles — the broadest/flagship label
- EMAZ/Fujinsha: Most extreme age bracket (50s–70s), 3 series
- EMBZ/Ranbukan: 400+ titles across two eras; SIBA/Ranbukan is the SM variant
- SOAV/Shiroto Harem: Amateur married woman infidelity documentary (from Feb 2015)
- BIJN/Bijin Majo: 200+ titles (vol. 1), 400+ (vol. 2)
**Mousouzoku absorption:** October 17, 2014. Part of Mousouzoku's 2014 consolidation phase. Operationally no change — labels continued under same codes. Database lists separately because internal organizational divisions persist within the umbrella.
**Casting model:** Primarily documentary/amateur style with real older women rather than signed idol actresses. Does not operate on the contract-actress model used by WILL's mainstream labels.
